About GEOWISE

Setting the standard for GIS excellence and enterprise IT solutions in India

GEOWISE is a leading IT solutions provider headquartered in India, specializing in transformative geographic information systems (GIS) and location-based intelligence solutions. With a commitment to excellence and innovation, we help enterprises across utilities, municipal bodies, and critical infrastructure maximize their operational efficiency and competitive advantage.

Our Mission: To empower organizations with advanced GIS technologies and intelligent data insights that drive strategic business decisions and operational excellence.

Our experienced team combines deep domain expertise with cutting-edge ESRI ArcGIS and open-source GIS technologies to deliver comprehensive solutions that integrate seamlessly with your enterprise systems. We don't just implement technology—we partner with you to understand your unique business challenges and craft solutions that create measurable value.

Core Services

Comprehensive GIS solutions tailored to your enterprise needs

🎯

GIS Consulting

Strategic planning and gap analysis to develop comprehensive GIS roadmaps. We assess your current systems, identify opportunities, and design solutions that align with your business objectives.

🛠️

ArcGIS Development

Custom application development using ESRI ArcGIS technologies. From web GIS solutions to enterprise implementations, we build robust systems that deliver location intelligence at scale.

🔓

Open Source GIS

Flexible, cost-effective solutions using GeoServer, QGIS, PostgreSQL, and OpenLayers. Perfect for organizations seeking open standards and community-driven innovation.

🔗

System Integration

Seamlessly integrate GIS with enterprise systems like EAMS, DMS, SCADA, and Oracle CC&B. Bridge location intelligence with operational management for unified insights.

📊

Data Management

End-to-end GIS data services including modeling, quality management, migration (ETL), and governance. Ensure your geospatial data is accurate, organized, and actionable.

🛡️

Support & Maintenance

Round-the-clock application support with ticketing management and SLA-based resolutions. Our experts ensure your GIS systems run smoothly and evolve with your needs.
